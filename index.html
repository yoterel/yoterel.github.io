About Me

Curently doing my masters studies in the in the Visual Computing & Applied Geometry lab at Tel-Aviv University, supervised by Dr. Amit H. Bermano. Interested in video & 3D geometric applications of deep learning. I've earned my bachelor's in Electric Engineering & Physics at Tel-Aviv University where I focused on computer architecture, semi-conductors and optics.

I'm also quite an experienced embedded software engineer - I worked in Valens Semiconductors, and in ART medical for a couple of years mainly implementing all sorts of algorithms in the firmware using C/C++.

More importantly, I'm a huge geek, and passionte about many engineering topics such as: brain machine interfaces, game engines, physically based rendering, projection mapping, procedural generation programming, and many more..

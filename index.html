About Me

Curently doing my phd studies in the Visual Computing & Applied Geometry lab at Tel-Aviv University, supervised by Dr. Amit H. Bermano. Interested in Graphics, Video & 3D geometric applications of deep learning.

Bachelor's from Faculty of Electric Engineering & Faculty of Exact Sciences (Physics) at Tel-Aviv University where I focused on semi-conductors, computer architecture and optics.

Masters from Faculty of Electric Engineering & School of Computer Science at Tel-Aviv University, under super vision by Dr. Amit H. Bermano and Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or (mostly computer-vision projects)

I also worked in Valens Semiconductors, and in ART medical for a couple of years mainly implementing all sorts of algorithms for their firmware using C/C++.

Most importantly, I'm a huge geek, passionte about many topics such as: brain-computer interfaces, game engines, physically based rendering, projection mapping, procedural generation programming, and many more..
